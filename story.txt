The famous business Ronald Gump was facing heavy backlash once more. He was planning on tearing down a low income housing project to build his world famous casino. Now he is faced with the major decision of the public's support or his casino building.
He then decides that it is better to respect the publics opinions and act with moral ethics. So then he decides to go ahead with the low income housing project. But when he decides to go ahead with the project, he is faced with enormous pressure from his peers and employees that this decision will make or break his companies future.
Faced with a daunting ultimatum, ronald finds a solution to the issue by selecting a more prime location to build his casino all the while making donations to the low income housing project to quash public hysteria. This way he gets the best of both worlds.
